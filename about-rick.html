A little about Rick:
Rick Bright was born in Glendale, California and grew up in Southern California. He attended California State University, Fullerton and graduated in 1991 with a degree in finance with a strong emphasis in real estate investment analysis and appraisal. In 2006, Rick obtained his real estate broker license.

Rick’s wife, Jennifer, is a Certified Public Accountant. They have been married 25 years and have two teenagers, ages 16 and 18.  They have enjoyed living in San Clemente for 12  years and are privileged to call San Clemente home.

Rick specializes in the South Orange County area and stays informed of all real estate offerings in Orange County.
